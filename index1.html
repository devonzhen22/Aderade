Our Story

Passionate About Inspiring Others

Adera De, Movement and Motiviation LLC is an on the go Dance, Fitness and Wellness Company. Our mission is to serve individuals of all ages, changing lives one at a time through movement and motivation to live a healthy and better lifestyle. The vision that we have at Adera De is to create family and community through these three components previously mentioned. We will offer technique dance classes such as Ballet, Jazz, Hip Hop, Modern and African; Social dance classes such as Zumba, Line Dancing and Ballroom. We also offer group fitness classes that include Boot Camp, Toning and Sculpting, Interval Training and Flexibility. Ballet Workout

Designed for participants who have no former dance training or experience.
The focus is to dance for fitness and health, to use the fundamentals of ballet to improve posture, muscle tone, balance, coordination, flexibility and body awareness

8 to 10 Sessions of classes focused on one technique (Ballet, Modern, Jazz African or Indian). Participants will receive complete exposure of technique followed by performance ready dance number
